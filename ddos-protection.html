  • Mitigation Techniques

    CoinsHost has a vast experience in protecting against application-level and network-level attacks. We host hundreds of servers on our protected network and mitigate against dozens of DDoS attacks every day. Our engineers have developed a custom built self-learning solution based on highly efficient hardware, proven mitigation techniques and proprietary software. CoinsHost will detect, automatically analyse and mitigate against HTTP, UDP, SYN, ICMP floods and all other types of DDoS attacks that scale up to 200 Gb/s.

  • Protection Setup

    There's no rocket science knowledge required on your side to set DDoS protection up. All you need to do is to point your domain's DNS A zones to the protected IP address that we'll provide you with, or just change your name servers to our name servers and let us do the rest. If you need assistance with this, we will adjust your DNS free of charge, as well as add any custom rules to the firewall. Although we start filtering traffic from the very first packet, please be advised that new DNS entries still take from few minutes to several hours to get propagated across all DNS servers on the web.
