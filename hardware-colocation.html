Colocation Setup and Equipment Delivery

Colocation setup procedure does not require your physical presence in the Data Center. There are to ways to deliver your equipment to our Data Center in Zurich - you can either bring your equipment to the DC yourself or arrange for a courier to collect and deliver it to us. Once we receive the equipment that you wish to co-locate, our engineers will install and set it up accordingly to your requirements.

Free DDoS Protection with every Colocation order

CoinsHost provides free protection against all types of DDoS attacks with every equipment colocation order. CoinsHost has a vast experience in protecting against application-level and network-level attacks. We host hundreds of servers on our protected network and mitigate against dozens of DDoS attacks every day. Our engineers have developed a custom built self-learning solution based on highly efficient hardware, proven mitigation techniques and proprietary software. CoinsHost will detect, automatically analyse and mitigate against HTTP, UDP, SYN, ICMP floods and all other types of DDoS attacks.
